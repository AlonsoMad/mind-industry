As AI agents and large context databases become central to enterprise operations, a fundamental question arises:

How can my agents trust my data if it is not consistent?

This project builds upon the foundational research in Multilingual Discrepancy Detection. Originally designed to detect cultural and factual discrepancies in multilingual QA systems, MIND's powerful AI engine has been repurposed to meet the industrial demand of purging and pruning large context DBs.

MIND provides a powerful, user-in-the-loop AI pipeline designed to systematically detect contradictions and discrepancies within text and contextual databases. Our mission is to prune and maintain contextual integrity within large context DBs.

By combining Polylingual Topic Modeling, hybrid retrieval, and advanced LLMs, we empower users to identify, review, and resolve inconsistencies. This ensures that the data feeding AI agents—whether in large corporate environments, technical documentation, or specialized knowledge bases—is trustworthy, factually accurate, and free of contradictions.
